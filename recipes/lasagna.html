Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of noodles, meat sauce, creamy cheese, and melted mozzarella. It is warm, hearty, and perfect for family meals.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

This recipe serves 6. Preparation time is about 20 minutes, and cooking time is around 40 minutes.

  1. Cook the noodles

    boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the meat sauce

    In a pan, cook ground beef with garlic until browned.

    Add pasta sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.

  3. Mix the cheese

    In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan.

  4. Layer the lasagna

    In a baking dish:

  • Spread meat sauce on the bottom
  • Add noodles
  • Spread cheese mixture
  • Sprinkle mozzarella
  • Repeat layers.

  2. Rest and serve

    Let lasagna cool for 10 minutes before cutting.
